  \frametitle{New Control Plots}
    \begin{block}{}
      \begin{itemize}
      \item Tighter version of cuts to look at region where trigger is 100\% efficient
      \item Cuts are:
      \item[-] met$>130$ $jet1_pt>50$, $dijet_deta>3.6$, metnomu\_significance$>3$, jetmetnomu\_mindphi$>1.5$, $jet_{1,2} \eta <4.7$, $jet_{1}\eta\cdot jet_{2}\eta<0,m_{jj}>=1200,jet_{2} p_{T}>50$
      \item met, $jet2 p_{T}$ and $m_{jj}$ cuts chosen to be above highest trigger threshold and at at least 50\% efficiency for run D trigger 
      \end{itemize}
    \end{block}
